What is KIRTI?

KIRTI stands for Kabir Inspired Rohana Trust Initiative - a spiritual, educational, and social movement based at the sacred Kabir Kuti, Rohana.

Founded to spread the timeless teachings of Sant Kabir to today's youth, KIRTI represents the perfect harmony of Faith + Wisdom + Action.

We believe in awakening the inner consciousness through the profound wisdom of Sant Kabir, creating a bridge between ancient spiritual knowledge and modern life challenges.

Our initiative serves as a beacon of hope, guiding young minds towards self-realization, compassion, and purposeful living.

Our Sacred Mission

Inner Peace

To awaken inner peace and purpose in young minds through the transformative teachings of Sant Kabir

Self-Realization

To teach self-realization, compassion, and timeless values from Sant Kabir's divine wisdom

Sacred Service

To offer a sacred space for learning, meditation, satsang, and selfless service to humanity

Sant Kabir's Divine Teachings

Self-Reflection

"Bura Jo Dekhan Main Chala, Bura Na Miliya Koye..."
Look within yourself, not at others' flaws. True transformation begins with self-reflection.

Patience & Peace

"Dheere Dheere Re Mana, Dheere Sab Kuch Hoye..."
Have patience in life. Everything happens in its own divine time with steady progress.

Living Wisdom

"Pothi Padh Padh Kar Jag Mua, Pandit Bhayo Na Koye..."
True knowledge comes from living values, not just reading books. Experience is the greatest teacher.
