What is KIRTI?

KIRTI stands for Kabir Inspired Rohana Trust Initiative - a spiritual, educational, and social movement based at the sacred Kabir Kuti, Rohana.

Founded to spread the timeless teachings of Sant Kabir to today's youth, KIRTI represents the perfect harmony of Faith + Wisdom + Action.

We believe in awakening the inner consciousness through the profound wisdom of Sant Kabir, creating a bridge between ancient spiritual knowledge and modern life challenges.

Our initiative serves as a beacon of hope, guiding young minds towards self-realization, compassion, and purposeful living.
